#FIGHT4POKER

The Poker Alliance (PA) is an LLC organization representing American poker players with the goal of establishing a safe and secure place to play poker. Formed in 2005 as the Poker Players Alliance, a non-profit membership organization of nearly 1 million poker players, the PA continues to serve as the unified voice on behalf of Americans’ right to play poker in all its forms.

Subscribe to the PPA mailing list. This is the best way to get the latest news and action alerts on the #fight4poker. Also, you can connect with the PPA on Facebook and Twitter. We are one of the best follows in the poker world. Not only will you get breaking news, but you will get daily updates on our activities and where we are making progress to bring iPoker to you. You will feel like a poker insider after a few days of following the PPA.

On Capitol Hill and in state houses across the country, the PA works with key lawmakers to raise awareness of the issues impacting the U.S. poker playing community and promote sensible public policies that benefit and protect all American consumers online and offline.

We can’t fight without your financial support. Contributing to PPA is the best bet in poker. Every dollar we raise goes directly to the effort to bring safe and fun online poker back to the U.S. Please support the PPA today so we have the resources to continue working hard on your behalf.

As the recognized expert on the game of poker, the PA is also an established legal resource, taking the argument that poker is a game of skill directly to state and federal courts.
